### Bengula Inc on LinkedIn

The public LinkedIn company page lists Bengula Inc with the slogan **Adding meaning to life** and frames the company around one core thesis:

> "Only businesses that use data to make decisions and put their services online are going to succeed."

The page describes Bengula Inc as a digital business-support brand that helps companies **collect relevant business stats, analyse them, and turn them into actionable insight to grow** — through data analysis, SEO, and digital marketing.

#### Public Profile Details
- **Industry:** Writing and Editing
- **Founded:** 2014
- **Company size:** 2-10 employees
- **Location:** Malindi, Coast 80200, Kenya
- **Specialties:** web content, product descriptions, blog posts, landing pages, and buying guides

#### How This Connects To Today
That original data-and-digital foundation is now one of two pillars at Bengula Inc. The second - **finance and banking advisory** - pairs the same evidence-led approach with practical access to the banking and capital tools a business needs to grow.
